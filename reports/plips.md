## About PLIPs

PLIPs (Plone Improvement Proposals) are feature proposals and enhancement requests that shape the future of Plone. 
They are tracked as GitHub issues with the label '03 type: feature (plip)' in the main Plone repositories:

- Products.CMFPlone
- plone.restapi
- volto
