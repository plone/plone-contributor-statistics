## Key Differences

### At the Top

**4-Tier separates**:
- Level 1 Elite (20+ PLIPs / 500+ PRs): 3 + 3 = ~5 people
- Level 2 Leaders (10-19 PLIPs / 200-499 PRs): 5 + 3 = ~8 people

**3-Tier combines**:
- Platinum (10+ PLIPs / 300+ PRs): 8 + 5 = ~11 people

**Advantage 4-Tier**: Recognizes exceptional outliers (tisto 61 PLIPs, sneridagh 1046 PRs) separately from leaders

**Advantage 3-Tier**: Larger prestigious top tier, less hierarchical

### In the Middle

**4-Tier**:
- Level 3 Active: 16 + 20 = ~31 people (5 overlap)

**3-Tier**:
- Gold: 16 + 9 = ~24 people (1 overlap)

**Difference**: 3-tier Gold includes only 100-299 PRs vs 4-tier includes 50-199 PRs, creating larger 4-tier middle

## Recommendations by Community Priority

### Choose 4-Tier If:

✅ **Elite recognition matters**
- Properly celebrates legends (tisto 61 PLIPs, sneridagh 1046 PRs)
- Creates aspirational peak tier
- Natural breaks in data (20 PLIPs, 500 PRs)

✅ **More granular progression**
- 4 clear steps for advancement
- Better distinguishes leaders (10-19) from active (3-9)

✅ **Smaller elite group acceptable**
- ~5 people at Level 1 (prestigious and exclusive)

### Choose 3-Tier If:

✅ **Simplicity is paramount**
- Easier to explain and communicate
- 3 tiers = Gold/Silver/Bronze familiar model

✅ **Larger top tier desired**
- ~11 people at Platinum (more inclusive leadership)
- Avoids making top tier too exclusive

✅ **Less hierarchical feel**
- 3 levels feel less corporate than 4

---

**Conclusion**: Both systems work well. 4-tier provides better granularity and proper elite recognition; 3-tier provides simplicity and larger leadership tier. Choice depends on community values.
