## Executive Summary

The Plone community adopts a **3-tier PLIP contributor level system** to recognize strategic contributions:

- 🏆 **Platinum**: Core Leaders (10+ PLIPs)
- ⭐ **Gold**: Active Contributors (3-9 PLIPs)
- 🌟 **Silver**: Emerging Contributors (1-2 PLIPs)

This simplifies the previous 5-tier system while maintaining clear progression paths and appropriate recognition for all contribution levels.

## Migration from 5-Tier System

### Old System → New System

| Old 5-Tier Level | Threshold | Contributors | New 3-Tier | Change |
|------------------|-----------|--------------|------------|--------|
| **Level 1** | 20+ PLIPs | 3 | 🏆 Platinum | ⬆️ Combined |
| **Level 2** | 10-19 PLIPs | 5 | 🏆 Platinum | ⬆️ Combined |
| **Level 3** | 5-9 PLIPs | 7 | ⭐ Gold | ⬇️ Partial |
| **Level 4** | 2-4 PLIPs | 15 | ⭐ Gold / 🌟 Silver | Split |
| **Level 5** | 1 PLIP | 33 | 🌟 Silver | ⬆️ Expanded |

### Specific Migrations

**Level 1 (20+) → Platinum**: 3 people
- ✅ All promoted to Platinum
- ✅ Maintain elite status within larger tier

**Level 2 (10-19) → Platinum**: 5 people
- ✅ All promoted to Platinum
- ✅ Join elite group as core leaders

**Level 3 (5-9) → Gold**: 7 people
- ✅ All remain in upper mid-tier
- ✅ Clear path to Platinum (need 1-5 more PLIPs)

**Level 4 (2-4) → Gold or Silver**: 15 people
- ✅ 9 people with 3-4 PLIPs → Gold
- ⬇️ 6 people with 2 PLIPs → Silver

**Level 5 (1) → Silver**: 33 people
- ✅ All remain in entry tier
- ✅ More inclusive threshold (1-2 vs just 1)

### Impact Summary

**Promotions**:
- 8 people now at Platinum (vs 3 at old Level 1)
- Larger, more prestigious leadership tier

**Consolidations**:
- Mid-tier clearer (Gold = 3-9 PLIPs)
- Entry tier more welcoming (Silver = 1-2 PLIPs)

**Benefits**:
- ✅ Simpler system (3 vs 5 tiers)
- ✅ Larger leadership group (8 vs 3)
- ✅ Clear progression (1-2 → 3-9 → 10+)
- ✅ More inclusive entry level

## Progression Paths

### Silver → Gold

**Requirement**: Submit 1-2 more PLIPs (reach 3 total)

**Timeline**: Typically 1-3 years

**Example Path**:
- Year 1: Submit first PLIP → Silver
- Year 2: Submit second PLIP → Still Silver (need 3)
- Year 3: Submit third PLIP → **Promoted to Gold** ⭐

**Support Available**:
- PLIP mentorship pairing
- Writing workshops
- Review process guidance
- Community feedback and encouragement

### Gold → Platinum

**Requirement**: Submit 1-7 more PLIPs (reach 10 total)

**Timeline**: Typically 3-5 years

**Example Path**:
- Years 1-3: Accumulate 3-5 PLIPs → Gold
- Years 4-7: Continue strategic contributions → 6-9 PLIPs (still Gold)
- Years 8-10: Reach 10 PLIPs → **Promoted to Platinum** 🏆

**Support Available**:
- Strategic planning committee participation
- Senior mentor pairing
- Cross-repository collaboration opportunities
- Conference speaking to build profile

### Maintaining Status

**Platinum & Gold**: Lifetime achievement
- Once achieved, status maintained
- Based on all-time PLIP count
- No degradation over time (strategic vision persists)

**Silver**: Entry level
- Welcoming and supportive
- Encouraged to continue contributing
- Clear path forward always visible

## FAQ

### Q: How are tiers calculated?

**A**: Based on total number of PLIPs submitted (all-time):
- 10+ PLIPs = Platinum
- 3-9 PLIPs = Gold
- 1-2 PLIPs = Silver

### Q: Do open/closed PLIPs matter?

**A**: No, all PLIPs count equally. Strategic proposals have value regardless of implementation status.

### Q: Can I lose my tier?

**A**: No. Tiers are based on all-time contributions and do not degrade. Once earned, always earned.

### Q: What if I submit PLIPs to multiple repositories?

**A**: All PLIPs count, regardless of repository. Cross-repository work is encouraged and valued.

### Q: How do I advance to the next tier?

**A**:
- Silver → Gold: Submit 1-2 more PLIPs (reach 3 total)
- Gold → Platinum: Submit 1-7 more PLIPs (reach 10 total)

### Q: When are tiers reviewed?

**A**: Continuously. As soon as you submit a PLIP that moves you to a new threshold, you're automatically promoted.

### Q: I was Level 1 (20+ PLIPs). Am I still recognized as elite?

**A**: Yes! You're now Platinum, part of the core leadership group. Your exceptional contribution (20+ PLIPs) is still recognized and celebrated within the Platinum tier.

### Q: What about code contributions (PRs)?

**A**: This system focuses on strategic contributions (PLIPs only). A separate system recognizes implementation work (PRs). You can achieve tiers in both!
